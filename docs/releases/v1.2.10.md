## SnapAnchor 1.2.10

SnapAnchor 1.2.10 redesigns Capture History to match the main application's modern light interface.

### Redesigned Capture History

- Cool light-blue window background with dark, high-contrast text.
- Large **Repeat last region**, **Open folder**, and **Clear history** header actions.
- Search, source, type, date, favourites, and recycle-bin controls grouped in one rounded white filter panel.
- Responsive three-column capture cards at the normal window size.
- Larger previews, clearer size/time metadata, compact name and context controls, and pastel Copy, Pin, Edit, OCR, Favourite, and Delete actions.

All existing history behavior remains available, including filtering, tags, favourites, context previews, recycle restore, permanent deletion, recordings, OCR, pinning, and editing.
