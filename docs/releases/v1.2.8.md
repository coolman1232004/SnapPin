## SnapPin 1.2.8

SnapPin 1.2.8 is a deliberately small release for testing the redesigned portable updater introduced in 1.2.7.

### Improvement

- The About page now clearly identifies the running edition as a **portable copy** or **installed copy**.
- **Copy version information** copies a concise summary containing the SnapPin version, edition, and process architecture.
- The new controls are available in English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese.

### Testing from portable SnapPin 1.2.7

Open **Preferences > About > Check now**, or restart SnapPin with automatic update checks enabled. SnapPin 1.2.7 should offer this 1.2.8 release through the new staged portable-update workflow.
